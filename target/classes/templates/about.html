Our Story

Founded in 2025, Salon Oski began with a simple mission: to revolutionize the salon experience by blending artistry with technology.

What started as a small studio in Diyathalawa has grown into a premier destination for hair and beauty, known for our personalized approach and expert stylists.

Our Mission

To empower you to look and feel your absolute best through innovative styling, premium care, and a touch of AI magic.
